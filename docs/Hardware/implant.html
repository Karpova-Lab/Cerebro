Head Implant Resources¶

Required Tools¶

  • Ruler
  • Soldering iron
  • Tweezers
  • Fiber gripper
  • Fiber stripper
  • Fiber cleaver
  • 3-axis fiber launch system
  • Power meter and photodiode sensor
  • Ultraviolet light

Assembly Instructions¶

Prepare Fiber¶

  1. Strip fiber and cut to length
  2. Cut off end of fiber to make a clean tip
  3. Cut to length ~16mm

Couple Fiber to Laser Diode¶

  1. Insert laser diode into connector
  2. Place connector in alignment jig
  3. Place fiber in alignment jig
  4. Turn on power meter
  5. Open up the Thorlabs’ Power Meter Software and adjust the following settings:
  • Range: 12mW
  • Wave: 520nm
  • Log Configuration
    • No of Samples: 11,000
    • Interval Time: 0.1
  1. Click Start Log
  2. Set the power meter to 8.0 volts with a current limit of 300 mA
  3. Provide power to the laser diode by pressing the “on/off” button
  4. While watching the power graph update, use the knobs to adjust the x,y,and z alignment of the fiber with respect to the laser diode.
  5. Apply Norland Optical Adhesive 68 to the fiber/laserdiode junction.
  6. Cure the adhesive using UV light

Combine PCB, Laser Diodes, and Fiber Guide¶

  1. Insert the ceramic ferrules into the acrylic fiber guide. The flat side of the ferrules should be flush with the face of the acrylic.
  2. Solder 1 leg of each Laser diode to the PCB
  3. Bend the laser diodes toward eachother so the tips of the fiber slightly intersect
  4. Cut ~1cm of heat shrink tubing and slide it onto one of the laser diodes
  5. Slide the fiber guide onto the two fibers
  6. Apply heat to the heat shrink tubing
  7. Suround the fibers and laser diodes epoxy
  8. Surround epoxy with large heat shrink tubing
