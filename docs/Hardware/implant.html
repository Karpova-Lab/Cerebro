Head Implant Resources¶

Required Tools¶

  • Ruler
  • Soldering iron
  • Tweezers
  • Fiber stripper
  • Fiber cleaver
  • 3-axis fiber launch system
  • Power meter and photodiode sensor
  • Ultraviolet light

Assembly Instructions¶

  1. Strip fiber and cut to length
  2. Align fiber to laser diode
  3. Apply Norland Optical Adhesive 68 and cure it with UV light
  4. Solder diodes at a slight angle to the implant PCB
  5. Laser cut fiber guide
  6. Insert ferrules into acrylic to create fiber guide
  7. Thread coupled fibers through fiber guide
  8. Incase laser diodes in epoxy
  9. Surround the implant with heat shrink tubing
