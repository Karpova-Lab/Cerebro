Head Implant¶

Required Tools¶

  • Ruler
  • Soldering iron
  • Tweezers
  • Fiber stripper
  • Fiber cleaver
  • 3-axis fiber launch system
  • Power meter and photodiode sensor
  • Ultraviolet light

Bill of Materials¶

Assembly Instructions¶

  1. Strip fiber and cut to length
  2. Align fiber to laser diode
  3. Apply Norland Optical Adhesive 68 and cure it with UV light
  4. Solder diodes at a slight angle to the implant PCB
  5. Surround the doides with 8mm tall shrink tubing
  6. Place phototransistor in the tubing and fill with epoxy
  7. Laser cut fiber guide
  8. Insert ferrules into acrylic to create fiber guide
  9. Thread coupled fibers through fiber guide
  10. Add more epoxy
  11. Surround the implant with heat shrink tubing
